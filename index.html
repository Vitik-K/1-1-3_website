About 1+1=3

In the fast-paced and interconnected world of today, the power of collaboration cannot be overstated. Often, the best solutions arise from the meeting of diverse minds, each bringing unique perspectives and skillsets to the table. With this ethos in mind, we are thrilled to announce the inception of a new student group at Camosun College: "1 + 1 = 3." This group is on a mission to dissolve academic boundaries and cultivate collaboration among students from varying backgrounds, majors, and departments.

In today's interconnected world, the need for cross-disciplinary collaboration has never been greater. "1 + 1 = 3" is breaking down the walls that often isolate students within their fields by offering a platform where technology enthusiasts and students from arts, sciences, business, and more can converge. Through workshops, seminars, and joint projects, the group aims to unleash the potential of combined efforts, inspiring students to experience the magic of teamwork and diversified thinking.

By encouraging students to blend their knowledge and skills, we're nurturing a generation of collaborative thinkers prepared to tackle multifaceted challenges head-on. As "1 + 1 = 3" gains momentum, we invite all students to participate, broaden their horizons, and witness firsthand the incredible results that occur when collaboration knows no bounds.
